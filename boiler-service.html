What We Tackle

Common Boiler Problems We Solve in Edinburgh

Edinburgh homes experience a range of boiler and heating issues. Here are the most common faults our customers call us about.

Boiler Not Firing Up

A boiler that won't ignite is often caused by a failed pilot light, faulty ignition lead, or a gas supply issue. Our engineers diagnose ignition faults quickly and carry common parts to get your heating back on fast.

Losing Pressure

A boiler that repeatedly loses pressure usually has a faulty expansion vessel, a worn pressure relief valve, or a small leak in the system. We find and fix the root cause rather than just topping up the pressure.

Radiators Not Heating

Cold radiators or hot at the top but cold at the bottom are often caused by sludge build-up. We offer power flushing, chemical treatment, and radiator repair in Edinburgh to restore full circulation.

Unusual Noises

Kettling, banging, or gurgling from your boiler indicates lime-scale build-up, air in the system, or a worn pump. We identify the source and carry out the appropriate repair to restore quiet, efficient operation.

High Energy Bills

An unserviced boiler works harder and uses more fuel. A professional annual service improves combustion efficiency and can bring your heating bills back down significantly.

Boiler Leaking

Water leaking from a boiler is caused by failed seals, corroded joints, or a cracked heat exchanger. Our leak detection and repair team inspects the source and carries out the correct fix — never a temporary patch.

Book a Boiler Service

Understanding Your Quote

What Affects the Cost of a Boiler Repair?

Boiler repair costs vary depending on what's wrong, the boiler type, and what parts are needed. We always diagnose first and quote clearly before carrying out any chargeable repair work.

For a straightforward annual service, we offer a fixed price — no surprises. For faults and repairs, the quote depends on the factors below.

Get a Quote
01

Fault Type

Simple issues like a faulty thermostat or pressure valve are quick and low cost. More complex faults — heat exchanger failure, PCB issues, or gas valve problems — take longer and cost more.

02

Boiler Make & Model

Parts vary in cost and availability by brand and model. Common brands like Worcester Bosch and Vaillant have readily available parts. Older or discontinued models may require sourcing, which can add time.

03

Parts Required

Labour is often a smaller proportion of the cost than parts. We'll tell you exactly what's needed and the cost of each part before ordering anything.

04

Accessibility

Boilers in awkward locations — inside cupboards, in loft spaces, or in Edinburgh tenement flats with limited access — may take longer to service or repair.

05

Emergency Callout

Same-day and out-of-hours emergency callouts carry a higher rate than scheduled appointments. If you have no heating or hot water, our emergency plumbing service can respond fast.

06

Overall System Condition

Heavily scaled, sludged, or corroded systems take longer to work on safely. If we find significant system issues during a boiler service, we'll advise you on options including radiator repair and power flushing.
